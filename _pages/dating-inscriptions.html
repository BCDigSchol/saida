This story map will take on a journey through the urban history of Saida (the old city). The story is told by the Inscriptions found on the city's historical buildings. These indeed are not the only inscriptions to be found in the city. The inscriptions mentioned herewith will tell you the story of Saida's Urban history, but it is a story that has more details awaiting to be revealed.
